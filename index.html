    What is Ethereum?

    Ethereum is a technology that's home to digital money, global payments, and appications. The community has built a booming digital economy, bold new ways for creatrs to earn online, and so much more. It's open to everyone, wherever you are in the world - all you need is the internet.

    A farier financial system

    Today, bilions of people can't open bank accounts, other have their payments blocked. Ethereum's decentralized finance (DeFi) system never sleeps or descriminates. With just an internet connection, you can send, recieve, borrow, earn interest, and even stream funds anywhere in the world.

    The interest on assets

    Ethereum isn't just for digital money. Anything you can own can be represented, traded and put to use as non-fungible tokens (NFTs). You can tokenise your art and get royalities automatically every rue its resold. Or use a token for something you own to take out a loan. Th possibilities are growing all the time.

    An open Internet

    Today, we gain access to 'free' internet services by giving up control of our personal data. Ethereum services are open by default - you just need a wallet. These are free and easy to set uo, controlled by you, and work without any personal info.

    A new frontier for development

    Ethereum and its apps are transparent and open source. You can fork code and re-use functionallity others have already built. If you don't want to learn language you can just interact with open source code using JavaScript and other existing languages.
